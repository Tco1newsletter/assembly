Grade 1 - Teamwork


Relationship Skills Assembly -Teamwork - “Communication is hard you see, but in the end, it is the key, to that great relationship, we all want and need.” The assembly was hosted by Grade 1 EFA students spreading the importance of building healthy relationships in a classroom. GRADE 2 - Resolving Conflicts

Relationship Skills Assembly -Resolving Conflicts

”A lot of misunderstandings stem from lack of communication !” A special assembly was conducted by class 2 afternoon students. They talked about the essence of the relationship and learnt the importance of relationship in our lives. They learnt to resolve conflicts by opening the knots created due to misunderstandings and keep their love and understanding knots tied up strong.

EFA Classes 3-5


At school, through our social emotional learning (SEL) classes, we aim to explicitly encourage our learners to cultivate constructive relationships with others critical to success in school and community setting. As an extension to our SEL sessions , EFA Classes 3-5 hosted a special assembly on the theme of Relationship Skills held on 11.11.2022. The story is about a very nervous dinosaur named Penelope Rex, who is starting the new school year in a classroom full of children. It's the first day of school for Penelope Rex, and she can't wait to meet her classmates. But it's hard to make human friends when they're so darn delicious! Despite her teacher's insistence that Penelope spit them out, and the firm declaration that classmates are not snacks, Penelope just can't resist. That is, until Penelope gets a taste of her own medicine and finds she may not be at the top of the food chain after all...By the end of the story, students quickly recognize the importance of being a good friend.
